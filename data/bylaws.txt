# HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION BYLAWS
## WILLOW CREEK COMMUNITY ASSOCIATION

### ARTICLE I - NAME AND LOCATION

**Section 1.1 - Name**
The name of this association shall be "Willow Creek Community Association," hereinafter referred to as the "Association."

**Section 1.2 - Location**
The principal office of the Association shall be located within the community or at such other place as may be designated by the Board of Directors.

### ARTICLE II - DEFINITIONS

**Section 2.1 - Association**
"Association" means the Willow Creek Community Association, a non-profit corporation.

**Section 2.2 - Board**
"Board" or "Board of Directors" means the governing body of the Association, elected by the members.

**Section 2.3 - Common Area**
"Common Area" means all real property owned by the Association for the common use and enjoyment of the members.

**Section 2.4 - Declaration**
"Declaration" means the Declaration of Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions recorded for the community.

**Section 2.5 - Lot**
"Lot" means any plot of land shown upon any recorded subdivision map of the community with the exception of the Common Area.

**Section 2.6 - Member**
"Member" means each person or entity who holds a membership interest in the Association.

### ARTICLE III - MEMBERSHIP

**Section 3.1 - Membership Requirements**
Every person or entity who is a record owner of a fee or undivided fee interest in any Lot shall be a member of the Association.

**Section 3.2 - Membership Rights**
Each member shall be entitled to one vote for each Lot owned. Membership rights are non-transferable except upon sale or transfer of the Lot.

**Section 3.3 - Membership Obligations**
Members are obligated to pay assessments and comply with all governing documents of the Association.

### ARTICLE IV - BOARD OF DIRECTORS

**Section 4.1 - Composition**
The Board of Directors shall consist of five (5) members, each of whom shall be a member of the Association or the spouse of a member.

**Section 4.2 - Terms of Office**
Directors shall serve two-year staggered terms. No director may serve more than two consecutive terms without a one-year break in service.

**Section 4.3 - Election Process**
Directors shall be elected by secret ballot at the annual meeting. Nominations may be made from the floor or by petition filed with the Secretary at least 30 days prior to the meeting.

**Section 4.4 - Powers and Duties**
The Board shall have the power to:
- Adopt and publish rules and regulations
- Fix the amount of annual and special assessments
- Hire and discharge managing agents and employees
- Maintain insurance on Association property
- Make repairs, additions and improvements to Common Area

**Section 4.5 - Meetings**
Regular meetings of the Board shall be held monthly. Special meetings may be called by the President or any two directors with 48 hours written notice.

**Section 4.6 - Quorum**
A majority of the Board members shall constitute a quorum for conducting business.

### ARTICLE V - OFFICERS

**Section 5.1 - Required Officers**
The officers shall consist of a President, Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer, elected annually by the Board from among its members.

**Section 5.2 - President**
The President shall preside at all meetings and shall be the chief executive officer of the Association.

**Section 5.3 - Vice President**
The Vice President shall act in the absence or disability of the President and shall assist the President in the performance of duties.

**Section 5.4 - Secretary**
The Secretary shall record the votes and keep the minutes of all meetings and proceedings of the Board and members.

**Section 5.5 - Treasurer**
The Treasurer shall receive and deposit all monies and shall keep proper books of account.

### ARTICLE VI - MEETINGS OF MEMBERS

**Section 6.1 - Annual Meetings**
The annual meeting shall be held in March of each year at a time and place designated by the Board.

**Section 6.2 - Special Meetings**
Special meetings may be called by the President, Board of Directors, or upon written petition of members representing 25% of the total votes.

**Section 6.3 - Notice of Meetings**
Written notice of all meetings shall be given at least 14 days prior to the meeting date.

**Section 6.4 - Quorum**
Members holding 30% of the total votes shall constitute a quorum.

### ARTICLE VII - ASSESSMENTS

**Section 7.1 - Assessment Authority**
The Association shall have the power to fix, levy, collect and enforce payment of assessments for common expenses.

**Section 7.2 - Annual Assessments**
Annual assessments shall be fixed by the Board and shall be payable in monthly installments on the first day of each month.

**Section 7.3 - Special Assessments**
Special assessments may be levied by the Board for capital improvements or extraordinary expenses with approval of members holding 67% of the total votes.

**Section 7.4 - Assessment Liens**
Unpaid assessments shall constitute a lien upon the Lot and may be foreclosed in the same manner as a mortgage.

**Section 7.5 - Late Fees and Interest**
Assessments not paid within 30 days of the due date shall incur a late fee of $25 and interest at 12% per annum.

### ARTICLE VIII - ARCHITECTURAL CONTROL

**Section 8.1 - Architectural Review Committee**
The Board shall appoint an Architectural Review Committee of at least three members to review all proposed modifications.

**Section 8.2 - Approval Required**
No exterior modifications, additions, or alterations to any Lot shall be made without prior written approval of the Committee.

**Section 8.3 - Application Process**
Applications must be submitted with detailed plans, specifications, and materials samples. The Committee shall respond within 45 days.

**Section 8.4 - Standards**
All modifications must be compatible with the architectural style of the community and maintain property values.

### ARTICLE IX - USE RESTRICTIONS

**Section 9.1 - Residential Use**
All Lots shall be used for single-family residential purposes only. No commercial activities are permitted except home offices that do not create traffic or parking issues.

**Section 9.2 - Rental Restrictions**
Lots may be rented for periods of not less than six months. Short-term rentals under 6 months are prohibited.

**Section 9.3 - Pet Restrictions**
A maximum of two pets per Lot is allowed. Dogs must be leashed when in Common Areas. Aggressive breeds as defined by the Board are prohibited.

**Section 9.4 - Vehicle Restrictions**
No commercial vehicles, boats, RVs, or trailers may be parked on streets or driveways for more than 48 hours without Board approval.

**Section 9.5 - Noise Restrictions**
Quiet hours are from 10 PM to 7 AM on weekdays and 10 PM to 8 AM on weekends. No construction activities are permitted during quiet hours or on Sundays.

### ARTICLE X - ENFORCEMENT

**Section 10.1 - Violation Procedures**
Upon notice of a violation, the Association shall send a written notice to the owner specifying the violation and requiring correction within 30 days.

**Section 10.2 - Fines**
Failure to correct violations may result in fines of $25 for the first offense, $50 for the second offense, and $100 for each subsequent offense.

**Section 10.3 - Hearing Rights**
Members have the right to a hearing before the Board before any fine is imposed.

**Section 10.4 - Legal Action**
The Association may pursue legal action to enforce compliance and recover attorney fees and costs.

### ARTICLE XI - INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**Section 11.1 - Association Insurance**
The Association shall maintain general liability insurance with minimum coverage of $2,000,000 per occurrence.

**Section 11.2 - Directors and Officers Insurance**
The Association shall maintain Directors and Officers liability insurance with minimum coverage of $1,000,000.

**Section 11.3 - Property Insurance**
The Association shall maintain property insurance on all Common Area improvements at replacement cost.

### ARTICLE XII - AMENDMENTS

**Section 12.1 - Amendment Process**
These Bylaws may be amended by a vote of members holding 67% of the total votes at a meeting called for that purpose.

**Section 12.2 - Notice Requirements**
Notice of proposed amendments must be given at least 30 days prior to the meeting and include the full text of proposed changes.

### ARTICLE XIII - DISSOLUTION

**Section 13.1 - Dissolution Authority**
The Association may be dissolved by vote of members holding 80% of the total votes.

**Section 13.2 - Asset Distribution**
Upon dissolution, assets shall be distributed to members in proportion to their assessment obligations.
